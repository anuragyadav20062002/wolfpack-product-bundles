**Screenshot:** `34-success-suite.png`

## Concept

The "Success Suite" is a cross-app bundle offering from **Skai Lama** — the parent company behind EB | Easy Bundle Builder. It packages multiple Skai Lama apps at a discounted combined price.

Tagline: *"Get More Apps for Less. Stop juggling multiple app subscriptions."*

---

## App Stack Included

| App | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Easy Bundle Builder** | Multi-step bundle builder (this app) |
| **Kite Discounts** | Advanced discount rules, custom discounts |
| **Fly Bundles** | Additional bundle types |
| **Checkout Wiz** | Checkout customizations, upsells (Shopify Plus) |
| **Giftkart** | Gift cards, store credits, cashbacks |

---

## Suite Plans

All plans show a crossed-out original price and a discounted price, suggesting bundle savings of ~40%.

| Plan | Revenue Cap | Monthly Price | Target |
|------|------------|---------------|--------|
| **Starter** | Up to $25k/month across all apps | $199/month ~~$349~~ | Stores with low cart value |
| **Growth** | Up to $50k/month | $399/month ~~$699~~ | Stores ready to scale |
| **Scale** | Up to $100k/month | $599/month ~~$999~~ | Shopify Plus stores |
| **Unlimited** | No cap + Dedicated Success Manager | $799/month ~~$1199~~ | Shopify Plus, no limits |

**Starter includes:**
- Bundling & discounting suite (Easy Bundle Builder)

- Landing page + product page bundle builders
- Mix & match bundles
- Quantity breaks
- Free gifts
- Tiered discounts
- Goal-based progress bar
- Shipping discount

**Scale adds:**
- Everything in Growth
- Checkout customizations (Checkout Wiz)
- Checkout upsells
- Custom discounts by Kite

- Checkout A/B testing

**Unlimited:**
- Everything in Scale

## Key Observations

- The Success Suite is a **platform play** — Skai Lama is building a multi-product ecosystem and using Easy Bundle Builder as the entry point
- Revenue-based caps (not feature gating) means merchants can grow through each tier naturally
- The cross-app pricing is structured so the bundle is meaningfully cheaper than buying each app individually — strong incentive to consolidate
- The content library (blogs + ebooks) on this page is used for both education and SEO — it positions Skai Lama as a thought leader in bundling strategy
- Checkout Wiz (Shopify Plus checkout customizations) is gated to Scale+ — this is likely Skai Lama's highest-margin product and is positioned as a Plus-tier differentiator
