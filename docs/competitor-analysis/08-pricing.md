## Pricing Model

Revenue-based tiers — price scales with the merchant's bundle sales volume, not feature gating. **All features are available on every plan.**

### Billing Cycles
- **Monthly** (default)
- **Yearly** — 15% discount

---

## Plan Tiers

| Plan | Bundle Sales Threshold | Monthly Price | Key Selling Point |
|------|----------------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Starter** | < $500/month | **Free** | Free until you grow |
| **Essential** | < $5,000/month | $49/month | Start bundling right now |
| **Plus** | < $20,000/month | $149/month | Priority onboarding + custom branding |
| **Enterprise** | < $75,000/month | $399/month | Custom builds + white-glove service |

- All paid plans: **30-day money-back guarantee**
- All paid plans: **15-day free trial**
- Both Essential and Plus show a "Get an extended trial" button (suggesting flexibility for trial extensions)

## Key Observations

- **Revenue-based pricing** removes the fear of over-paying when starting — the free tier creates zero-risk adoption
- **No feature gating** is a strong differentiator vs. competitors who lock features behind higher plans. Every merchant gets the full product from day one.
- The **"Get an extended trial"** CTA is a real-time sales tactic — it suggests the app team is willing to extend trials to reduce churn, implying direct sales involvement
- The $49 → $149 → $399 pricing curve is steep at the top end — there's a significant jump from Plus to Enterprise, which may push high-volume merchants to negotiate
- The affiliation/partner program is a B2B growth channel for agencies building on behalf of clients
