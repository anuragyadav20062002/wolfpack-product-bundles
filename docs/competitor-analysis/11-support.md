## Support Channels

| Channel | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Live Chat (24/7)** | Intercom — *"reply and assist instantly"* — also proactively opened with a message from "Sid" on first load |
| **Scheduled Meeting** | "Schedule Meeting" button with assigned account manager ("Surya") — one-on-one onboarding/support calls |
| **Help Center** | *"Find answers to your questions in our help center"* — links out to docs site |

Every merchant on the free plan already has a **named account manager** displayed on the support page — this is a high-touch retention tactic even at zero revenue.

## Key Observations

- The **named account manager** with a photo (Surya) creates a personal relationship at scale — even free-plan merchants feel like they have a dedicated contact
- **24/7 live chat** is a strong support commitment for an app at this price point; it likely drives the review rating
- The proactive Intercom message (from "Sid") on first load reduces the friction of reaching out — many merchants won't initiate contact, so the app initiates for them
- The FAQ content maps directly to the most common onboarding blockers (publishing, products not showing, image cropping) — these are likely derived from real support tickets
- The dual CTA pattern ("View Demo" / "Learn More") on each bundle type allows both hands-on learners and readers to engage in their preferred way
